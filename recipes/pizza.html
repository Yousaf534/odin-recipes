Pizza Recipe

How to Make Pizza

Pizza is a fan favorite dish! Who doesn't like to hang out with friends and enjoy a slice of fresh and delicious pizza? Here is an amazing recipe to make a fresh pizza for your family and friends!


Ingredients

  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 1 lb. fresh mozzarella, sliced thin and patted dry
  • 3/4 c. pizza sauce
  • 4 oz. thinly sliced pepperoni or chorizo
  • Minced fresh rosemary
  • Shredded parmesan cheese
  • Vegetable oil
