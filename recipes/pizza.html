Pizza Recipe

How to Make Pizza

Pizza is a fan favorite dish! Who doesn't like to hang out with friends and enjoy a slice of fresh and delicious pizza? Here is an amazing recipe to make a fresh pizza for your family and friends!


Ingredients

  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 1 lb. fresh mozzarella, sliced thin and patted dry
  • 3/4 c. pizza sauce
  • 4 oz. thinly sliced pepperoni or chorizo
  • Minced fresh rosemary
  • Shredded parmesan cheese
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the back of a sheet pan with flour. Prepare toppings and pour vegetable oil into a bowl.
  2. Press the dough out into a thin rectangle. Pull the dough up and sprinkle more flour underneath.
  3. Prepare the grill for high direct heat. rub oil onto grill grates. Carefully slide pizza dough onto the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Flip the pizza dough. Brush pizza dough with olive oil and spread on the pizza sauce. Top it with mozzarella and pepperoni. Close the grill and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Transfer pizza to a cutting board. Sprinkle with rosemary and Parmesan cheese.
