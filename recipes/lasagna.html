Lasagna Recipe

How to Make Lasagna

Lasagna is a very delicious dish that everyone should learn how to make. It is easy to make and can be fun for the whole family to enjoy. Here is the recipe to making the most delicious lasagna you will ever have!


Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef.
  • One onion.
  • Two cloves of garlic.
  • One can of crushed tomatoes.
  • Two cans of tomato sauce.
  • Two cans of tomato paste.
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar.
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles.
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • One egg.
