Cheeseburger Recipe

How to make a Cheeseburger

Cheeseburgers are an American staple. You can always rely on a cheeseburger to satisfy your cravings because it has all you need! Tune in to how to make the most delicious cheeseburger from the comfort of your own home!


Ingredients

  • 5 oz beef patty
  • Sliced cheese of your choice
  • Soft white buns with sesame seeds
  • Lettuce, Tomatoes, Pickles, Onions, and any other vegetables you like
  • Sauce of your liking (Ketchup, Mustard, Mayonaisse)

Steps

  1. Form patties and salt them.
  2. Preheat oil in a heavy-based cast iron skillet over high heat.
  3. Place patty on skillet for 1 1/2 minutes and gently press down with a spatula. Flip the patty.
  4. Place cheese on the patty and cook for another 1 1/2 minutes.
  5. Toast the buns.
  6. Smear the buns with your choice of sauce.
  7. Place vegetables of your choice onto the buns.
  8. Place patty on bun and top it off with the top bun.
  9. Enjoy your delicious cheeseburger!
