Cheeseburger Recipe

How to make a Cheeseburger

Cheeseburgers are an American staple. You can always rely on a cheeseburger to satisfy your cravings because it has all you need! Tune in to how to make the most delicious cheeseburger from the comfort of your own home!


Ingredients

  • 5 oz beef patty
  • Sliced cheese of your choice
  • Soft white buns with sesame seeds
  • Lettuce, Tomatoes, Pickles, Onions, and any other vegetables you like
  • Sauce of your liking (Ketchup, Mustard, Mayonaisse)
